# Add another Series.

1. Add a second Series to our scatter plot. Our X-Axis is body mass, Column H. Our Y-Axis is flipper length, Column E.

2. Create a trendline.

3. Create an Y-Axis title.

4. Click the X-Axis. Find _Axis Options_. Adjust the minimum bound and maximum bound to the appropriate level.

5. Click the Y-Axis. Find _Axis Options_. Adjust the minimum bound and maximum bound to the appropriate level.
