# Box-n-Whisker

1. In our _Insert_ tab, click the chart icons, specifically _Box and Whisker_.

2. If we embedded it inside our `penguins_raw` worksheet, move it to another worksheet.

3. Our x-values focus on species, Column A.

4. Our y-values focus on flipper length, Column E.

5. Create a Chart Title.

6. Create a X-Axis Title.

7. Create a Y-Axis Title.

8. In our Y-Axis, Find _Axis Options_. Adjust the minimum bound and maximum bound to the appropriate level.
